# Privacy Policy & GDPR Compliance

**Last Updated:** November 3, 2024

## Introduction

HARVEST (the "Application") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data.

## Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is the organization hosting this HARVEST instance. For questions about data processing, please contact your system administrator.

## Data We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Collected when you contribute data or register as an admin
- **Usage Data**: Session information, timestamps, and interaction logs
- **Contributed Content**: Literature annotations, entity relationships, and research data you submit

### Technical Data
- **Session Cookies**: Used to maintain your login state and preferences
- **Browser Session Storage**: Temporary storage for search history and UI preferences
- **IP Address**: May be logged by the server for security and troubleshooting purposes

## Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and process your personal data for the following purposes:

1. **Authentication**: To verify your identity and manage access to administrative functions
2. **Attribution**: To track contributions and maintain data provenance
3. **Communication**: To contact you regarding your contributions or administrative matters
4. **Service Improvement**: To enhance the Application's functionality and user experience
5. **Security**: To protect against unauthorized access and ensure data integrity

## Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we process your personal data based on:

- **Consent**: When you voluntarily provide information (e.g., submitting contributions)
- **Legitimate Interest**: For security, fraud prevention, and service improvement
- **Contractual Necessity**: To provide the services you request

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- Data is stored on secure servers managed by your organization
- Email addresses may be hashed for display in the Browse interface
- Session data is stored temporarily in your browser

### Security Measures
- Data transmission is protected using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS)
- Access to administrative functions requires authentication
- Regular security updates and monitoring
- Email addresses displayed in Browse interface are hashed to protect privacy

### Data Retention
- **Active Contributions**: Retained as long as they remain relevant to the research project
- **Session Data**: Automatically cleared when you log out or close your browser
- **Admin Credentials**: Stored securely with password hashing
- **Usage Logs**: May be retained for up to 12 months for security and troubleshooting

## Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

### Right to Access
You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.

### Right to Rectification
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

### Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
You can request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.

### Right to Restrict Processing
You can request that we limit how we use your personal data.

### Right to Data Portability
You can request a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

### Right to Object
You can object to certain types of data processing, including processing for direct marketing.

### Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw your consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

## Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact your system administrator or the data controller for your HARVEST instance. We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by GDPR.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize data sharing
- **Legal Compliance**: When required by law or legal process
- **Service Providers**: With trusted partners who help us operate the Application (e.g., hosting providers)

### External API Usage

The Application may use external APIs for enhanced functionality:

- **Semantic Scholar**: For academic paper search (no personal data shared)
- **arXiv**: For preprint repository access (no personal data shared)  
- **OpenAlex**: For open catalog of scholarly papers (email recommended for polite pool, no personal data shared)
- **Web of Science**: For citation database access (requires API key, no personal data shared)
- **Unpaywall**: For open access PDF discovery (email required per API terms)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:
- [Semantic Scholar Privacy Policy](https://www.semanticscholar.org/product/api)
- [OpenAlex Documentation](https://docs.openalex.org/)
- [Unpaywall Data Policy](https://unpaywall.org/faq)

## Cookies and Browser Storage

### Session Cookies
- Used to maintain your login state
- Essential for Application functionality
- Deleted when you log out or close your browser

### Local Storage
- Used for UI preferences and search history
- Can be cleared via your browser settings
- Does not contain personally identifiable information

## International Data Transfers

If data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as:
- Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission
- Adequacy decisions by the European Commission
- Binding Corporate Rules

## Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will:
- Notify affected individuals within 72 hours as required by GDPR
- Report the breach to the relevant supervisory authority
- Take immediate steps to mitigate any harm

## Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through:
- Application notifications
- Email to registered users
- Updates to this document with a new "Last Updated" date

## Contact Information

For questions, concerns, or to exercise your rights:

- **Data Protection Officer**: Contact your organization's DPO
- **System Administrator**: Contact your HARVEST instance administrator
- **Supervisory Authority**: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

## Compliance Commitments

We are committed to:
- Processing data lawfully, fairly, and transparently
- Collecting data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes
- Ensuring data accuracy and keeping it up to date
- Storing data only as long as necessary
- Implementing appropriate security measures
- Demonstrating compliance with GDPR principles

## Technical and Organizational Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure data security, including:

- **Access Control**: Role-based access restrictions
- **Encryption**: Data in transit and at rest
- **Logging**: Audit trails for administrative actions
- **Backup**: Regular data backups with secure storage
- **Testing**: Regular security assessments and updates

## Data Protection by Design and Default

The Application is designed with privacy in mind:
- Email hashing in public-facing Browse interface
- Session-based storage for temporary data
- Configurable field visibility for administrators
- Minimal data collection by default
